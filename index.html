About me

John Erler is a enrollee of the Georgia Tech Boot Camp, a leading bootcamp that is in the greater Atlanta area.

John comes has a bachelor of science in chemistry, and he comes from a background of environmental chemistry, specializing in air and water analysis for contaminates.

John has been working to transition into the space of software development. He has taken the Harvard cs50 class and has striven for being a creative, flexible, and driven student in this endeavor.

While not learning to develop code, he enjoys reading books, watching the Mandolorian, and cooking. He has a side project of quality testing board games that friends produce. One day he inspires to make his own boardgame.
